Privacy Policy

Consumer Roleplay Simulator — Medi Solutions Insurance Agency

Effective: June 1, 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how the Consumer Roleplay Simulator ("the Tool") collects, uses, and stores information submitted during agent training sessions. By using this Tool, you agree to the practices described below.

1 Who We Are

The Consumer Roleplay Simulator is operated by Medi Solutions Insurance Agency, a division of Alliant Insurance Services, for the purpose of internal agent training and performance evaluation.

All data collected through this Tool is managed by Brian DeRoss (brdeross@fjins.net).

2 What Information We Collect

When an agent completes a roleplay session, the following information is automatically submitted for review:

  • Agent's full name (provided voluntarily before the session begins)
  • Persona and scenario type used in the session
  • Performance scores (Rapport, Discovery, Control, Clarity, Objection Handling, Confidence, Opportunity Recognition, Transition Attempt, Overall Score)
  • Whether a cross-sell opportunity was recognized and pursued
  • Key coaching points and a transcript summary generated by the simulator

No personally identifiable consumer data is collected. All consumer personas are fictional and simulated.

3 How We Use This Information

Session data is used exclusively for:

  • Internal agent training and performance tracking
  • Coaching and feedback by agency management
  • Identifying skill gaps and improving training materials

Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

4 Data Storage

Session results are submitted to an internal tracking system accessible only to authorized agency administrators. Data is retained for internal training records and may be reviewed by agency management at any time.

5 No Consumer Data Collected

This Tool does not collect, store, or transmit any data from actual Medicare consumers or clients. All roleplay personas are entirely fictional. No real beneficiary information is ever entered or processed through this Tool.

6 Your Rights

Agents who have submitted session data may request a copy of their records or ask for data to be removed by contacting the administrator directly. Requests will be honored within a reasonable timeframe.

7 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Tool after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
